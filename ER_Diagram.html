Key Statistics

  • Total Tables: 20+
  • Primary Keys: All tables have unique identifiers
  • Foreign Keys: 20+ relationships defined
  • Database: exams_lms
  • Charset: utf8mb4 (Unicode support)
  • Engine: InnoDB (Transactions, Foreign Keys)

Entity Relationships

  • Users is the central entity, connected to most other tables
  • 1-to-Many relationships:
    • Role → Users (one role, many users)
    • Trade → Users (one trade, many users)
    • Subject → Study Materials
    • Exam → Exam Questions, Exam Attempts
    • Community Post → Comments, Reports
